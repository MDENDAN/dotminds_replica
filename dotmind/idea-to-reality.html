Idea To Reality!

This service is for people who have an idea of the product and want to create its digital picture. At dotminds we are your digital partners, we help you create your idea to reality. At dotminds, we want you to focus on your core business and we take care of the digital presence for your business. First we discuss about idea and get information from you and your views about the idea. Then we do a market analysis(form a set of multiple choice questions) for the idea to get the result from people what they think about the idea. After that we proceed to help you figure out what is the best platform for you (APP/ Website/ both). Project planning is done and mockup of the UI/UX is designed simultaneously. After Approval of design we proceed with planning of front end and back end planning and development. After Development phase we start with Testing of the product. After the product is tested thoroughly, the product is deployed. After Deployment with provide AMC and Digital Marketing Service for your business to gain visibility.
